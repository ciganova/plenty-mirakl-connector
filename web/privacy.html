Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-05-14. Controller: Vagabond Consulting LLC, 30 N Gould St Ste R, Sheridan WY 82801, USA. Contact: contact@vagabond-consulting.com.

1. What we collect

  • Account data: business email, company name, billing address, payment method (handled by Stripe).
  • Integration credentials: Mirakl API keys, Plenty REST credentials. Stored encrypted at rest.
  • Order metadata: order IDs, line items, shipping addresses, tracking numbers — only as needed to synchronise between Mirakl and Plenty.
  • Operational logs: sync timestamps, error messages. No request bodies containing customer-PII are persisted beyond 14 days.

2. This website

This marketing site is static and sets no cookies. It loads Tailwind CSS and HTMX from public CDNs (jsDelivr / unpkg) — those CDN providers may log your IP per their own policies. No analytics, no tracking pixels, no fingerprinting.

3. Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

Processing is based on (a) contract performance for the Service itself, (b) legitimate interests for operational logging and abuse prevention, (c) legal obligation for tax/accounting retention.

4. Sub-processors

  • Hetzner Online GmbH — hosting, EU (Germany).
  • Stripe, Inc. — payments.
  • Mirakl S.A.S. and Plenty Markets GmbH — only as the platforms you direct us to call on your behalf.

5. Retention

Account data is kept for the duration of your subscription plus 6 months. Invoicing data is kept for 10 years per US/EU accounting requirements. Sync logs: 14 days. You may request earlier deletion subject to legal retention obligations.

6. Your rights (GDPR Art. 12–22)

Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection. Email contact@vagabond-consulting.com. You may also lodge a complaint with your national data-protection authority.

7. International transfers

Operational data is processed on EU servers. Limited administrative access from the United States may occur on the basis of Standard Contractual Clauses.
